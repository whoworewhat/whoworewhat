Lewis Hamilton: F1 Champion to Met Gala Co-Chair!

From seven-time Formula 1 world champion to fashion icon, Lewis Hamilton is set to co-chair the 2025 Met Gala. Known for his bold style and advocacy for diversity, Hamilton's new role showcases his growing influence beyond racing.

Alexandra Saint Mleux: The New Face of Rhode!

Alexandra, one of the most beloved F1 WAGs, is the new face of Hailey Bieber’s skincare brand, Rhode. With her effortless beauty and growing influence, she perfectly embodies the brand's minimalist, inclusive ethos.

Sonam Kapoor Becomes Dior’s New Global Ambassador

Sonam Kapoor has been named Dior's new global ambassador, a major milestone in her fashion journey. With her impeccable style, she joins the ranks of global icons, bringing Indian influence to the luxury fashion world.

Emma Corrin and Miu Miu Drop a Bold Gender-Fluid Collection!

Emma Corrin has partnered with Miu Miu to launch a gender-fluid capsule collection that challenges conventional fashion boundaries. The collection includes tailored suits, oversized shirts, and stylish accessories for all genders.

Rihanna’s Savage X Fenty Expands with Adaptive Lingerie Line

Rihanna’s inclusive lingerie brand, Savage X Fenty, has unveiled a new line aimed at further expanding its commitment to diversity and accessibility. The collection, which launched this week, features adaptive lingerie designed specifically for people with disabilities.

Red Carpet Royalty: Carlos & Charles Steal the Show at Gladiator 2!

Both Carlos and Charles brought their individual styles to the red carpet, but what stood out most was how they complemented each other. Carlos’ classic elegance balanced out Charles’ fashion-forward statement, creating a dynamic duo that represented the diverse ways modern men are embracing fashion.
